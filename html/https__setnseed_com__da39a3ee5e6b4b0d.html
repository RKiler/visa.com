Have you noticed any improvements in efficiency, productivity, or results since incorporating the Set-N-Seed into your planter routine?

We have seen tremendous efficiency increases using this product. On a 16-row planter, you can correct the depth settings in 10-15 minutes or less. And that is a huge advantage especially in-season when growers are having depth issues due to incorrect settings. For example, a customer of ours had hydraulic downforce on his planter and had everything set correctly as far as electronics, so our precision team came out and did a quick depth true-up with set n seed. The customer had variations on every single row due to parts wearing out differently. Up to a 1/2” on some rows as far as variation. How would you say the Set-N-Seed has improved efficiency and productivity with planters?

We know that using even John Deere’s training, we can double-check their findings and tell our customers that actually, you need to be setting your row unit closer to this from the beginning. That’s not bashing John Deere, it’s just that iron can fluctuate. The settings can fluctuate and one-row unit might be different than another one, you know might have more weight than another.
